# 😋 Chomper

**Race the Sun** — a solo, dice-eating puzzle game that runs entirely in your browser. One file, no build step, no dependencies.

> ©2020 Sneaky Pirates · by Gustavo "Scary" Tontini · **v3**

## Play

Open `chomper.html` in any modern browser. That's it — desktop or mobile, works offline.

## How it works

Hop your Chomper across a hex meadow, eating dice for points, and reach a **Haven** by the Cave before the rising sun seals it shut.

- **Chomp** a glowing die to hop onto it; your **steps** refuel to that die's value (faces 1–6).
- **Stash** each eaten die into your **belly** (15 slots). Line up colours that match the slot for bonus points — and build trios and quads for more.
- **Belly full?** Burn a die (+1 step) to make room.
- **Burn 🔥** any belly die anytime for an extra step when you're short.
- **Race the sun** ☀ — every chomp advances it one row. Get caught and you're sunburned; let it reach the Cave and you lose.
- **Combos, cravings, and a per-board target** reward smart, greedy routes.
- The view **auto-zooms out for free** when nothing's in reach, so you're never forced to pay to look around.

Full rules are in-game (📖 Rules).

## Features

- Pure HTML5 canvas — no frameworks, no install.
- Customizable Chomper: 6 colours + accessories (glasses, shades, top hat).
- Local scoreboard, sound effects, and a fresh randomized board every run.
